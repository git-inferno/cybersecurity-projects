# GRC Portfolio — Nithin
Cybersecurity GRC projects demonstrating risk management, compliance, and policy skills.
## Projects
### 1. SMB Risk Register & Heat Map
**Scenario:** Fictional small business (Bright Dental Clinic)  
**Framework:** NIST RMF / ISO 27005  
**Tools:** Excel  
Identified 10 cybersecurity risks, rated by likelihood and impact, mapped to a 5×5 heat map, and proposed mitigations prioritized by risk score.
**Artifacts:** `GRC_Risk_Register_SmallBusiness.xlsx`

### 2. NIST CSF 2.0 Self-Assessment Scorecard
**Scenario:** Fictional healthcare supply company (HealthBridge Medical Supplies)  
**Framework:** NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0  
**Tools:** Excel  
Assessed 56 subcategories across all 6 CSF functions (Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover). Scored current vs. target maturity tiers, identified high-priority gaps, and built a prioritized remediation roadmap with owners and timelines.
**Artifacts:** `NIST_CSF2_Scorecard_HealthBridge.xlsx`
